The Why

Building Diversity in Oklahoma STEM through Visibility and Connection

In the STEM industry, it can prove difficult to find people of diverse backgrounds then connect without an existing network. ION offers tangible resultes for networking. Additionally, ION capitalizes on an opportunity for increasing fucture diversity in STEM by providing upcoming generations a means to find role models. This next-generation needs role models they can identify with, who have shared experiences. This project will not solve many of the problems in STEM regarding the lack of diversity. However, ION will provide a tool in connecting diverse individuals and representing diversity in the Oklahoma STEM community.
